Question 1

What would you expect the code below to print out?
numbers = [1, 2, 2, 3]
numbers.uniq

puts numbers

Answer: numbers will print 1, 2, 2, 3
uniq does not modify numbers and creates a new array with uniq numbers [1, 2, 3,]
